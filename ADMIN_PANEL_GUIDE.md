## Quick Start Guide

### What You Can Do Now

1. **Add Products/Services**
   - Click the green **"+ Add Product"** button
   - Fill in the product details
   - Upload images using the image upload component
   - Click "Save" to add to your website

2. **View Your Site**
   - Click **"View Site"** in the top right to see your website
   - Products you add will appear on the homepage

3. **Manage Existing Products**
   - Once you add products, they'll appear in cards below
   - Use **"Edit"** to modify products
   - Use **"Delete"** to remove products
